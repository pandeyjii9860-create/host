About Our Company

About Dewaki Construction Company

Founded in 2020, Dewaki Engineering Solutions is a leading construction and engineering firm based in Kathmandu, Nepal. We specialize in civil engineering, architectural design, and infrastructure development. With a focus on innovation and quality, we have delivered diverse projects, including commercial buildings, residential developments, and public infrastructure, contributing to Nepal’s growth.

Our commitment to excellence, safety, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for property owners, developers, and government institutions. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver outstanding results.
